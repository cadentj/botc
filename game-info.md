## Townsfolk

**Washerwoman** — You start knowing that 1 of 2 players is a particular Townsfolk.

**Librarian** — You start knowing that 1 of 2 players is a particular Outsider. (Or that zero are in play.)

**Investigator** — You start knowing that 1 of 2 players is a particular Minion.

**Chef** — You start knowing how many pairs of evil players there are.

**Empath** — Each night, you learn how many of your 2 alive neighbours are evil.

**Fortune Teller** — Each night, choose 2 players: you learn if either is a Demon. There is a good player that registers as a Demon to you.

**Undertaker** — Each night\*, you learn which character died by execution today.

**Monk** — Each night\*, choose a player (not yourself): they are safe from the Demon tonight.

**Ravenkeeper** — If you die at night, you are woken to choose a player: you learn their character.

**Virgin** — The 1st time you are nominated, if the nominator is a Townsfolk, they are executed immediately.

**Slayer** — Once per game, during the day, publicly choose a player: if they are the Demon, they die.

**Soldier** — You are safe from the Demon.

**Mayor** — If only 3 players live & no execution occurs, your team wins. If you die at night, another player might die instead.

---

## Outsiders

**Butler** — Each night, choose a player (not yourself): tomorrow, you may only vote if they are voting too.

**Drunk** — You do not know you are the Drunk. You think you are a Townsfolk character, but you are not.

**Recluse** — You might register as evil & as a Minion or Demon, even if dead.

**Saint** — If you die by execution, your team loses.

---

## Minions

**Poisoner** — Each night, choose a player: they are poisoned tonight and tomorrow day.

**Spy** — Each night, you see the Grimoire. You might register as good & as a Townsfolk or Outsider, even if dead.

**Scarlet Woman** — If there are 5 or more players alive (Travellers don't count) & the Demon dies, you become the Demon.

**Baron** — There are extra Outsiders in play. [+2 Outsiders]

---

## Demons

**Imp** — Each night\*, choose a player: they die. If you kill yourself this way, a Minion becomes the Imp.

---

\* Not the first night.

---

## First Night Order

1. Minion info *(7+ players only)*
   - If more than one Minion, they all make eye contact with each other.
   - Show the "This is the Demon" card. Point to the Demon.

2. Demon info *(7+ players only)*
   - Show the "These are your minions" card. Point to each Minion.
   - Show the "These characters are not in play" card. Show 3 good character tokens not in play.

3. **Poisoner** — The Poisoner points to a player. That player is poisoned.

4. **Spy** — Show the Grimoire to the Spy for as long as they need.

5. **Washerwoman** — Show the character token of a Townsfolk in play. Point to two players, one of which is that character.

6. **Librarian** — If there are any Outsiders in play, show the character token of an Outsider in play and point to two players, one of which is that character. Otherwise, show the hand signal for "zero".

7. **Investigator** — Show the character token of a Minion in play. Point to two players, one of which is that character.

8. **Chef** — Show the hand signal for the number of pairs of evil players neighbouring each other.

9. **Empath** — Show the hand signal (0, 1, 2) for the number of evil alive neighbours of the Empath.

10. **Fortune Teller** — The Fortune Teller points to two players. Show "yes" or "no" for whether one of those players is a Demon (or the red herring).

11. **Butler** — The Butler points to a player. Mark that player as "Master".

---

## Other Nights Order

1. **Poisoner** — The previous poisoned player is no longer poisoned. The Poisoner points to a player. That player is poisoned.

2. **Monk** — The previous protected player is no longer protected. The Monk points to a player (not themself). Mark that player "Protected".

3. **Spy** — Show the Grimoire to the Spy for as long as they need.

4. **Scarlet Woman** — If the Scarlet Woman becomes the Demon, show the "You are" card, then the Demon character token.

5. **Imp** — The Imp points to a player. That player dies. If the Imp chose themself, replace the character of a Minion player with a spare Imp token and wake the new Imp.

6. **Ravenkeeper** — If the Ravenkeeper died tonight, they point to a player. Show that player's character token.

7. **Undertaker** — If a player died by execution today, show that player's character token.

8. **Empath** — Show the hand signal (0, 1, 2) for the number of evil alive neighbours of the Empath.

9. **Fortune Teller** — The Fortune Teller points to two players. Show "yes" or "no" for whether one of those players is a Demon (or the red herring).

10. **Butler** — The Butler points to a player. Mark that player as "Master".

# Trouble Brewing — Helper Tokens

## Townsfolk

**Washerwoman** — 2 tokens:
- 1 "Townsfolk" token to mark the player who is actually the shown Townsfolk
- 1 "Decoy" token to mark the false player

**Librarian** — 2 tokens:
- 1 "Outsider" token to mark the player who is actually the shown Outsider
- 1 "Decoy" token to mark the false player

**Investigator** — 2 tokens:
- 1 "Minion" token to mark the player who is actually the shown Minion
- 1 "Decoy" token to mark the false player

**Fortune Teller** — 1 token:
- 1 "Decoy" token to mark the good player who always registers as a Demon to the Fortune Teller (the red herring)

**Monk** — 1 token:
- 1 "Protected" token to mark the player they choose each night (safe from the Demon tonight)

**Butler** — 1 token:
- 1 "Master" token to mark the player they choose each night (the Butler may only vote if this player is voting)

---

## Outsiders

**Drunk** — 1 token:
- 1 "Is the Drunk" token placed on whichever Townsfolk token the Drunk player believes they are (reminds Storyteller this player's ability doesn't work)

---

## Minions

**Poisoner** — 1 token:
- 1 "Poisoned" token to mark the player they choose each night (this player's ability malfunctions tonight and tomorrow day)

---

## Demons

**Imp** — 1 token:
- 1 "Die" token to mark the player they choose to kill each night

---

## Characters With No Helper Tokens

**Townsfolk:** Chef, Empath, Undertaker, Ravenkeeper, Virgin, Slayer, Soldier, Mayor

**Outsiders:** Recluse, Saint

**Minions:** Spy, Scarlet Woman, Baron
